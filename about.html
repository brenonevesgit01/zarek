About Zarek

Modern menswear, considered down to the last stitch.

Our Story

Zarek was founded in Lisbon in 2018 with a single, stubborn idea: that the best menswear should outlast the season it was made for. We started in a small studio above a tailor's shop, working with mills and ateliers our founders had known for decades.

A few years and a lot of patient iteration later, our pieces are worn in over thirty countries — but the studio is still in Lisbon, the mills are still the same, and the tailors downstairs are still the first people to see every prototype.

Our Philosophy

We believe in fewer, better things. Every Zarek piece is designed to be worn for years — not styled, photographed, and forgotten. That means slower design, longer development, and an obsessive focus on the materials and construction that determine how a garment ages.

It also means transparent pricing. We tell you what our pieces cost to make, what they cost to ship, and what we charge — so the value is yours to judge, not ours to sell.

Our Values

The three principles that shape every decision we make.

Quality

Materials sourced from mills we have worked with for years, and construction inspected piece by piece. Built to be repaired, not replaced.

Timeless Design

Considered silhouettes, restrained palettes, and a refusal to chase trends. Pieces designed today that will still feel right in ten years.

Honest Pricing

We publish what our pieces cost to make and what we mark them up. No artificial discounts, no inflated full prices — just fair value.
